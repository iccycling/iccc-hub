Essential Road Cycling Clothing and Where to Buy

Frederik Beck · 18 June 2025 · 1 min read

Getting the right kit makes a big difference — not just for comfort, but for safety.

The Essentials

  • Helmet — Non-negotiable. Get a well-fitting helmet with CE/CPSC certification.
  • Padded shorts — The single biggest comfort upgrade. Wear them without underwear.
  • Jersey — Back pockets for food and phone, fits close so it doesn’t flap.
  • Gloves — Protect your hands in a fall and reduce road vibration.

Where to Buy

  • Decathlon / Btwin — Best value for beginners.
  • Wiggle / Chain Reaction Cycles — Wide selection, frequent sales.
  • Club kit — Watch the news for ICCC kit drops at discounted rates.

Layering for British Weather

A good base layer plus windproof gilet covers most conditions. Arm warmers are more versatile than a long-sleeve jersey.
