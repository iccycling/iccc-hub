**26 Sep 2026:** New Study Year Begins — Welcome Back!

ICCC · 3 July 2026

Summer is over and a new study year begins — a bit of a bittersweet moment.

The days are getting shorter, which isn’t great news for cyclists. But with a bit of luck, October will still bring some good riding weather. And more importantly, the new study year brings fresh opportunities in London.

A special welcome to all the Freshers and everyone new at Imperial — an exciting new chapter starts now! If you’re interested in cycling, feel free to reach out to us in advance, ideally via Instagram. We’re happy to answer questions, or you can simply introduce yourself. Otherwise, come find us at the Freshers’ Fair!
