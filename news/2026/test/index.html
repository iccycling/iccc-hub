Spring is Here — Cycling Season Starts Now

ICCC · 5 March 2026

The clocks go forward on 29 March — and with it, the cycling season properly begins. Evenings get longer, temperatures creep up, and suddenly that post-lecture ride is actually possible again.

If you’ve been meaning to join but haven’t made it out yet, now’s the time. All levels welcome — just show up.
