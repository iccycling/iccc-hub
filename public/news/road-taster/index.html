Road Tasters Kick-off Ride & Social

Frederik Beck · 30 September 2025

The Road Tasters kick-off ride is happening Wednesday 1 October — a relaxed group ride for new members who want to try road cycling with the club for the first time.

Meet at Beit Quad at 6pm, ride to Richmond Park, do a couple of laps, and head back. Total distance around 25km. No experience needed, all bikes welcome.

Afterwards there’s a quick social at the union bar.
